  1. Fill in your details above, then click Copy Signature to Clipboard.
  2. Open Outlook (desktop or web) and go to
    Settings → Signatures → New Signature.
  3. Give your signature a name (e.g. SLG Standard).
  4. Click into the signature body and press Enter twice to add two blank lines — this gives you space above the signature when composing emails.
  5. Paste with Ctrl + V. Your formatted signature will appear.
  6. Check the Set default for new messages checkbox so your signature is applied automatically to all new emails.
  7. Click Save.
Use Chrome or Edge for this tool — other browsers may not preserve the signature formatting when copying.
